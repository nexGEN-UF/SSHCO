As any other non-profit organization, we can only survive by our volunteers. The mission of the SSCHO can only thrive through the passion of the community and the people who wish to help carry out its mission. Ways to help include, but are not limited to:

1) Spread our mission – Tell a Friend! Let those around you know about SSCHO and together we can transform healthcare in South Sudan.

2) Organize an Event – Schools, companies and neighborhoods across the nation have organized numerous events to benefit SSHCO. Contact us to find how we can help you organize in your community.

3) Follow us – Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin and other social networking sites.
