Mission Statement

To provide medical facilities, education in healthcare and sanitation, and to assist in the provision and delivery of healthcare supplies. We bring health and hope to where it is lost.

Goals

Our most immediate goal is to continue treating and offering healthcare to those who visit our clinic in Maar.

We are looking to form partnerships and establish lasting funds so all of our patients receive the highest quality medical attention.

In a country with only 85 South Sudanese doctors, the access to care is limited. With the proper partnerships and funding, we hope to provide healthcare education and scholarships to those pursuing the medical field.

Pre- and post-natal care is severely lacking but desperately needed. Our future clinics will focus on maternal healthcare to provide the best future for these women and their children.
